Privacy Policy for LinkedIn Time Filters

Last updated: August 29, 2025

Data Collection

This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or LinkedIn user data.

Local Storage

The extension stores only:

  • User preference settings (enabled/disabled state)
  • Last used time filter selection
  • Anonymous usage statistics (which filters are used most often)

All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API. No information ever leaves your computer.

Third Party Access

No data is shared with third parties. The extension operates entirely on your device and does not communicate with external servers.

LinkedIn Integration

The extension only:

  • Reads job posting timestamps to provide filtering functionality
  • Injects additional filter options into LinkedIn's existing interface
  • Uses LinkedIn's own filtering parameters (f_TPR) for compatibility

We do not access, store, or modify any LinkedIn user data, job applications, or personal information.
